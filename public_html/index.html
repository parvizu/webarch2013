About me

Hello everybody!

I am a first year masters student, and used to be a Data Analyst & Management Consultant from Mexico but I like to consider myself as a Web Developer at heart. After spending some time working in Mexico and the United States I decided it was time to continue my studies; actually I felt it was kind of a now or never moment for me.

So my goal is to find an area that has a little bit of both from time to time.
